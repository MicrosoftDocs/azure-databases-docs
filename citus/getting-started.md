# Getting started

Citus is an extension to PostgreSQL that distributes your data and your queries across a database cluster, to give your application performance at any scale.

> [!NOTE]  
> There are three important new capabilities in Citus that you should know about:
>
> - Columnar storage
> - Citus on a single node
> - Open source shard rebalancer
